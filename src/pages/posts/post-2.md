Chinese Indonesian Festival (CHIFEST) is a Chinese-Indonesian cultural celebration held at Ma Chung University. The series of events at CHIFEST 2025 is also very diverse. CHIFEST features an Asian Wave Party, K-pop dance performances, cosplay, and a DJ. The Festival Band by Mafest will showcase Gaines, Roman Century, Settleblaze, and the Ma Chung Music Club. The highlight of the event is a special performance by Hivi!. In addition, visitors can also participate in or watch a variety of Mandarin language competitions such as storytelling, short video, quiz bowl, children's coloring, and a fashion show. There are more than 50 culinary tenants offering both halal and non-halal menus, ranging from traditional to contemporary food.
